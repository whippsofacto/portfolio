Projects

inPlant -- Domestic Plant Monitoring System

inPlant is designed as a domestic plant monitoring system. Sensors are used to collect data from the environment surrounding the plant. This system utilises a soil moisture sensor and an ambient light sensor which are placed in the room, around the plant. Temperature data is sourced from the Yahoo temperature choreo, provided by Temboo. Sensed data is then available as output to Telegram messaging app users.

Rapunzel – Retro-futuristic Retelling of Grimm's Fairy Tale

A dystopian version of the dark, original story of Rapunzel in which a young woman finds herself locked in a government facility after the unlawful demise of her parents. In this Orwellian present society has never been more polarised. Set against the backdrop of revolution and told through a series news reports, radio broadcasts and personnel logs, this version of Rapunzel catalogues a prisoner’s struggle for freedom, a government’s desire for power and a country’s fight for liberty.
