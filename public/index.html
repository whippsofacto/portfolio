"Projects"

inPlant -- Domestic Plant Monitoring System

Using a light sensor, soil moisture sensor and the Yahoo Weather Choreo, readings are taken periodically from the plant environment. If the plant has dry soil or if the temperature has dropped low enough to inhibit growth, no bother! Using the Telegram messaging app the user can keep track of how their plant is faring and tend it accordingly.

Rapunzel -- Retro-futuristic Retelling of Grimm's Fairy Tale

A dystopian version of the original story in which a gifted young woman finds herself seized by The State after the loss of her parents. Set in a polarised, Orwellian society, against a backdrop of revolution this version of Rapunzel catalogues a prisoner’s struggle for freedom, a government’s desire for power and a country’s fight for liberty.
